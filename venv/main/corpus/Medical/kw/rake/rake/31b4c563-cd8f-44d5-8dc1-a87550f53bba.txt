Aneurysms associated with AVM's are classified as related to arterial feeder, intranidal, and unrelated.  The incidence ranges from 2.7% to 58%.    Most patients (80%) bleed from their aneurysm as opposed to the AVM.  If the source of bleeding is found to be the aneurysm, endovascular treatment of the aneurysm is performed first, followed by staged treatment of the AVM.

41 y.o. woman presents with progressive onset of bilateral arm pain and upper extremity weakness.
This patient is known to have Neurofibromatosis Type 2.  NF2 patients develop multiple Schwannomas, Meningiomas, and Ependymomas.   Which has led to the acronym of M.I.S.M.E. for:
M - multiple
I - inherited
S - Scwhannomas
M - meningiomas - and -
E - ependymomas

47 year old woman with headache, fever, nausea and vomiting for two days prior to admission.
Outside CT scan revealed a subdural collection - thought to be an empyema, prompting referral to our hospital.  No attempt was made to open transverse and sigmoid sinus due to the delay in initial diagnosis and rapid clinical deterioration.

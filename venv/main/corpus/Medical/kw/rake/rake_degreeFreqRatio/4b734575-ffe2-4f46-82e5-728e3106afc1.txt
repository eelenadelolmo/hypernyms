C-7 Clay Shoveler's Fracture and C-2 extension teardrop fracture
none
20 year-old man with neck pain after motor vehicle accident
In addition to the Clay shoveler's fracture there is an extension teardrop fracture of C2.

Extension teardrop fracture of C2: Avulsion of the anteroinferior corner of axis from hyperextension of neck. Vertical height of fragment greater than width. 

Clay shoveler's fracture: Oblique avulsive fracture through the spinous process. Hyperflexion fx at C7>C6>T1.

AP view: double ghost sign from displacement of the C-7 fracture fragment.

Non-contributory
Conservative therapy
MRA
1. Absent signal left ICA at skull base.
2.  Reconstitution of intradural ICA directly from the basilar  artery.

CT Scan
No bony carotid canal on the left.
Congenital Absence of the Internal Carotid Artery
None
55 year old man with history of an old right MCA territory infarction.  Underwent MRI and MRA to assess for worsening migraine headaches
Direct communication of the Basilar artery to reconstitute the intradural ICA has not been reported in the radiology literature.  Based on the MRA this not a Trigeminal artery or a Posterior Communicating artery.

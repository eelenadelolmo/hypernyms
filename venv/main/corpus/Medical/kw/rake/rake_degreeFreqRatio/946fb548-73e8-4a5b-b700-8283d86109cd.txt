Elevated urinay catecholamine byproducts
Enhancing mass in the prevertebral soft tissues near the bifurcation of the aorta on CT/MR which corresponds to the activity seen on MIBG scan.  The mass has high signal on T2 and is relatively homogeneously enhancing on post-gad T1 imaging.

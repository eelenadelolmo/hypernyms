Vertebral Fracture secondary to Osteomyelitis
Chronic rheumatoid steroid user presents with spinal osteomyelitis, previous history of pneumonia 2 months prior and minor fall 1 week prior.  Erosive changes and pathologic fracture of pedicle near T11-12, cord compression and paraspinal soft tissue involvement.

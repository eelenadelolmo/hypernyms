The vascular nodule sign is a secondary sign of atelectasis of the left lower lobe.  Three or more nodular densities along the left cardiac border signify the medial and anterior displacement of pulmonary vessels that accompany left lower lobe collapse.

Lacombe, P., Lallemand, D., Garel, L. and Sauvegrain, J.  "Pulmonary Vascular Nodules: New Sign of Left Lower Lobe Collapse in Children."  AJR.  139: 873-878, November 1982.

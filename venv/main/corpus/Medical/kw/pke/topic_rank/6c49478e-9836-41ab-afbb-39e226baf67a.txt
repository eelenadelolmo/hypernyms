Diffuse enlargement of the uterus.  Marked expansion of junctional zone, replaced with diffuse low T1 and T2 signal.  Small scattered foci of increased T2 signal represent small endometrial cysts, hemorrhage or endometrial tissue. The small ovoid mass in posterior uterine fundus, with low T2 signal, represents a fibroid.
Adenomyosis
Irregular menses

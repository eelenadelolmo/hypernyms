The US may be critical in differential diagnosis of SDH.  In benign enlarged subarachnoid spaces, US  demonstrates the cortical veins (draining veins) pass through the SAS and are not displaced by the fluid collection.  In a subdural collection, the veins will be pressed against the cortex; and, they will be displaced away from the inner table of the skull.
